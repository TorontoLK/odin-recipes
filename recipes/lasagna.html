Lasagna

Description:

Throw some ground beef, some lasagna noodles and cheese together. What you get is an awesome dish!

But if you really want to make something special, follow the recipe for big smokes famouse lasagna!

Ingrediants:

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb ground pork
  • 800g of mozzarella cheese
  • carrots
  • cellery
  • salt
  • pepper
  • lemon juice
  • a little love

Steps:

  1. slowly cook the meat for 2 hours in a pot
  2. mix with carrots, cellery, pepper, lemon juice
  3. boil the noodles until half cooked
  4. layer the noodles in a pan, and add meat mix
  5. cover pan in tin foil and bake for 2 hours
  6. bon appetite
