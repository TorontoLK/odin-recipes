Pizza Pie Oh My

Description

Don't miss out on this pizza recipe - A meat lovers delight!

Ingrediants

  • Sliced Salami
  • Sliced prosciutto
  • 800g of buffalo mozzarella cheese
  • fresh italian pizza dough
  • honey
  • pizza suace
  • sea salt
  • sun dried tomatos
  • dried cranberries
  • as always, a little love

Steps

  1. cook the pizza sauce for 1 hour
  2. roll out the dough and create a pizza shape
  3. add a thin layer of pizza sauce
  4. add cheese in 100g balls
  5. layer the salami, and prosciutto
  6. add the sundried tomatos, cranberries and dash of sea salt
  7. bake in oven until bottom is golden brown
  8. remove from oven and add honey
  9. thats a pizza pie!
