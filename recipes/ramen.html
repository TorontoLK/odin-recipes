The Royal Ramen

Description

If you thought Mr. Noodle was good, wait till you try this exquesite ramen recipe!

Ingrediants

  • Chicken breast
  • green onion
  • chicken broth
  • shaved carrots
  • two eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • corn
  • chilli flakes
  • ramen noodles
  • as always, a little love

Steps

  1. cook the chicken breast and slice into thin pieces
  2. boil the broth
  3. soft boil two eggs
  4. add salt, pepper and chilli flakes
  5. add carrots
  6. add corn
  7. add chicken
  8. add noodles
  9. once noodles are cooked to correct level, top with eggs, chicken breast and green onion.
  10. enjoy!
